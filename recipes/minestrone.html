Minestrone Soup


Description

A vegetable-packed soup that pairs well with toasted buttered bread or a chewy baguette to soak up the deliciously rich tomato-based broth.

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 minced white onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped zucchini
  • 1/2 cup frozen cut italian green beans
  • 1/4 cup minced celery
  • 4 tsp minced garlic
  • 7 cups water
  • 4 tsp vegetable bouillon
  • 1 can red beans, drained
  • 1 can small white beans, drained
  • 1 can petite diced tomatoes (not drained)
  • 4 oz can tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrot
  • 2 Tbsp parsley
  • 1 & 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1 & 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp basil
  • 1/4 tsp thyme
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 4 cups baby spinach
  • 1/2 cup small shell pasta or ditallini pasta

Steps

  1. Heat 3 Tablespoons olive oil over medium heat in a large soup pot.
  2. Saute onion, celery, garlic, green beans, and zucchini in the oil for 5 minutes or until the onions begin to turn translucent.
  3. Add water and bouillon to pot, plus tomatoes, tomato paste, beans, carrot, and spices.
  4. Stir well and bring the soup to a boil, then reduce heat and allow to simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Add spinach and uncooked pasta noodles. Cook for an additional 20 minutes.
  6. Optional: Serve with toasted and buttered Artisan Bread.
